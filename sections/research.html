Research Interests

Climate Change & Alpine Flora

Investigating the resilience and adaptation of high-altitude plant communities in the Himalayas in response to rising global temperatures and changing precipitation patterns.

Predator-Prey Dynamics

Studying the complex interactions within trophic cascades, particularly focusing on large carnivores and their regulatory effects on herbivore populations in protected areas.

Soil Microbiome & Forest Health

Analyzing the role of soil microbial communities in nutrient cycling, disease resistance, and overall resilience of tropical dry deciduous forests in Central India.

Human-Wildlife Coexistence

Developing data-driven models and community-based strategies to mitigate conflict and promote coexistence between human populations and wildlife in shared landscapes.
